Welcome to DataScience for Development.

DataScience for Development is a course to give non-technical international development experts basic literacy in the tools and techniques of data science. It's geared heavily towards international development and humanitarian data science, and since it was designed as a 3-day intensive course, has many links to help people continue their journeys.
